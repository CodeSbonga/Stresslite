Who We Are

Stresslite was born from a simple idea: students deserve technology that understands their unique challenges.

From Student Struggles to Smart Solutions

Founded in 2023 by a group of former university students, Stresslite was created out of personal experience. We knew firsthand the overwhelming pressure of balancing academics, social life, and personal wellbeing.

After graduating, we realized that with the right tools and technology, these challenges could be significantly reduced. That's when we set out to create Stresslite Watches - smart devices specifically designed to help students manage their time, reduce stress, and maintain healthy relationships.

Based in Johannesburg, South Africa, our team is passionate about supporting the next generation of leaders and professionals through innovative technology solutions.

Our Core Beliefs

What drives us every day to create better solutions for students

Our Mission

To empower university students with smart technology that helps them achieve academic success while maintaining healthy lifestyles and relationships. We believe technology should enhance human potential, not replace it.

Our Vision

A world where every student has the tools they need to thrive in university and beyond, without sacrificing their wellbeing or personal connections. We envision campuses where stress is managed, not endured.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the Stresslite story

Jan 2022

The Idea Was Born

After struggling through their own university experiences, our founders identified the need for a student-focused smartwatch that truly understands academic life.

Jun 2022

First Prototype

The initial Stresslite prototype was developed in a dorm room at Wits University, using off-the-shelf components and custom software.

Nov 2022

University Pilot

We partnered with three South African universities to test our prototypes with real students, gathering invaluable feedback.

Mar 2023

Company Founded

Stresslite was officially registered as a company with a dedicated team of 8 passionate individuals.

Sep 2023

First Product Launch

The Stresslite Watch 1.0 was launched to great acclaim, selling out within two weeks at campuses nationwide.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Stresslite

At Stresslite, our values are more than just words—they're the foundation of everything we do. We believe that academic success shouldn't come at the expense of personal wellbeing, which is why we've created technology that actively promotes balance. Every feature in our watches reflects our commitment to student empowerment, from the stress-monitoring algorithms developed with campus psychologists to the time-management tools designed alongside university students. We're not just selling smartwatches; we're fostering a movement that redefines what it means to thrive in university life. By prioritizing both productivity and self-care, we're helping South African students rewrite the narrative that academic achievement requires constant sacrifice—because at Stresslite, we know true success comes from harmony, not hardship.
