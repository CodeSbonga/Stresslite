About Stresslite

Our mission to help students achieve balance in their academic and personal lives.

Our Story

Stresslite was founded in 2023 by a group of former university students who struggled with the same challenges today's students face - balancing demanding academic schedules with maintaining healthy social lives and managing stress.

After graduating, we realized that with the right tools and technology, these challenges could be significantly reduced. That's when we set out to create Stresslite Watches - smart devices specifically designed to help students manage their time, reduce stress, and maintain healthy relationships.

Based in Johannesburg, South Africa, our team is passionate about supporting the next generation of leaders and professionals through innovative technology solutions.

Our Mission

To empower university students with smart technology that helps them achieve academic success while maintaining healthy lifestyles and relationships.

Our Vision

A world where every student has the tools they need to thrive in university and beyond, without sacrificing their wellbeing or personal connections.

Our Values
