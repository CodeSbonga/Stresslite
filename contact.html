Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Stresslite products and services

How do I purchase a Stresslite Watch?

You can purchase directly through our website, at select university bookstores, or through our campus representatives. We offer student discounts when you register with your university email address.

What's the warranty period for Stresslite Watches?

All Stresslite Watches come with a 12-month manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. The warranty does not cover accidental damage or normal wear and tear.

How does the stress monitoring feature work?

Our watches use advanced sensors to measure heart rate variability (HRV), which is a reliable indicator of stress levels. When elevated stress is detected, the watch will suggest breathing exercises or remind you to take a break.

Can I sync the watch with my university's calendar?

Yes! Stresslite Watches integrate with most university calendar systems. Simply connect your university account through our mobile app to automatically sync your class schedule and important dates.
